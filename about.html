Hi, I am Militsa

I’m a digital designer interested in how design shapes atmosphere, meaning, and cultural experience. My work explores editorial design, visual identity, and storytelling, with a focus on culture, art, and contemporary visual language.

I’m particularly drawn to creating work that feels intentional and immersive—where typography, imagery, and layout come together to communicate more than just information. Through my projects, I aim to translate ideas into visual narratives that are both aesthetically considered and conceptually driven.
